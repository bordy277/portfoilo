like an oven




like an oven hot sauce is designed to put a fire in the pants of a younger crowd, but appeals to anyone and everyone when it comes to the taste bud department. focusing on the three delicious sauce flavors, like an oven comes complete with three homepage ideas, brand poster, branding booklet, and flavor themed stationary system.

claire's




claire's is a rebranding campaign for the popular retail store – claire's. the campaign comes with a new logo, new approach to marketing, new audinence, advertisements, a mobile application, and more.

project go



project get outside, inc.(project go) is a non-profit organization founded in 2006 to assist Minnesota communities in connecting children to nature exploration and outdoor learning during the out-of-school hours.

I, along with a peer by the name of Anna Gretzinger, created a responsive website with an integrated mapping tool for the organization. The site allows users to interact with a map, look at photos from excursions, learn more about the environment that surrounds them, and can help answer any questions a user may have.

check it out at project go website




conservis



conservis corporation is a company that I spent one summer interning with. The company was going through a rebrand and I was hired to help with the transition. conservis offers agriculture management products that strive to streamline the farming process. During my timwe at conservis, I worked with two designers – Travis Oslon and Ben Hribar - who are credited with all of the design assets for the brand.

I had numerous responsibilities at conservis which included design meetings for new products, communications with out-sourced development team, and redesigning company material. I used the new design assets to redesign the back office website that I also coded a prototype for which can be found at this link
back office prototype

earthworks rising



earth works risingis an interactive badge system & website for young people that connects science, history, culture and art through a multidisciplinary approach that honors an authentic native american voice. the content being developed during the first phase of this project focuses on the earthworks of the Ohio river valley.

I, along with a team of peers – Yahya Nik Rushdi, Alix Nichols, Kristen Torres, Cole Spiess – created a prototype website for the organization.

check it out at earthworks website




sleepr




sleepr is a sleep-aid application for your iPad. It allows the user to create a relaxing and comfortable environment in their own bedroom. Includes ambient noises, alarm clock, and sleep timer. so lay back, listen up, and dream away.

font finder




font finder is an easy to use smart-phone application that is used to identify fonts and typefaces. font finder uses the image of a font to scan the internet for a match. It will supply a brief history of the font as well as where to find it on the internet. font finder is for everyone and anyone who wants to broaden their knowledge of fonts.

sirocco




It’s a hot, yet breezy day on the lake and you have a thirst for an alcoholic beverage. bio

hello, my name is brody steineck. I am 23 years of age and hail from a city known as woodbury, mn.

I will be a graduate of the university of minnesota-twin cities this december with a b.f.a. in graphic design. throughout my education, internships and freelance work, I have strengthened my graphic design skills with a focus on user experience. recently, I have been directing my efforts towards building my web development skills. development is a field that I have grown passionate about and is an area that I would like to continue to expand in.

I really enjoy films and good television shows. I enjoy everything about them. If I ever have free time that is what I will be doing. along with movies, I enjoy hanging out with my puppy nox, cooking, and a good whisk(e)y. I am looking to make as many connections in the twin cities as possible and always welcome new friends. please use my contact info to reach me if you ever need anything, or if you just like to talk!
